## Product / Brand Terms

### Gumloop
- Type: Brand name
- Means: One word, capital G. Never "GumLoop" or "Gum Loop".
- Status: 🔒

### Agent
- Type: Product term
- Means: The autonomous AI workflow Gumloop creates. Prefer over "bot", "workflow", "automation".
- Status: 🔒

---

## Words to Avoid

- "Bot" → use "agent"
- "Workflow" in user-facing copy → describe what it does
- "Seamlessly" → generic filler
- "Leverage" → corporate speak
- "Platform" when you can say what the agent does
- "Auto-do" → wordplay without meaning (retired from Shopify poster round)
