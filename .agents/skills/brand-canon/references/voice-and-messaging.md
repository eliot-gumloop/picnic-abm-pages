## Marketing Voice & Content Rules

> See **`gumloop-marketing-brand`** skill for complete guidelines. Key principles:

### Brand Vibe (from CEO Marketing Memo)
- **Authentic** (top priority)
- **Entertaining**
- **Approachable**
- **Thoughtfully produced**

### Voice Rules
- 🔒 **Talk like a friend** — no "camera voice," no buzzword double-speak
- 🔒 **Show real use cases only** — never fake a use case
- 🔒 **Be succinct** — edit ruthlessly, information-dense
- 🔒 **No overly-corporate language** — no walls of text, use analogies and plain language
- 🔒 **Write what you'd want to read** — if it's boring to you, it's boring to the audience

### The Litmus Test
Before publishing anything, ask: **"Would Cursor / Notion / Anthropic / Linear / Figma / Valve put this out?"**

### Quality Bar
Aspirational companies: Cursor, Notion, Ramp, Anthropic, Linear, Browser Company, Figma, Valve.

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Brand / marketing | Bold, punchy, one-liner statements |
| Presentation slides | Short scene-setting, never more than 2 short paragraphs |
| Announcements | Short header + 1–2 brief supporting paragraphs |
| Social posts | Authentic, entertaining, succinct — never force a post just to post |
| Feature launches | Real use cases, no hype, genuine excitement |

---

## Say this / Not that

| Say | Not |
|-----|-----|
| "AI agents" | "AI automation pipelines" |
| "Your teammate" | "The bot" |
| "Built by your team" | "Deploy and manage your infrastructure" |
| "Gumloop" | "GumLoop" / "Gum Loop" |
| Real, genuine excitement | Corporate buzzwords or overly-polished hype |
| Short, information-dense | Walls of text with redundant explanations |

---

## Content Principles

- 🔒 **Never fake a use case** — the product is good enough; genuine excitement and honesty come through
- 🔒 **Edit ruthlessly** — people's time is precious
- 🔒 **Visual thinking** — use screenshots, diagrams, illustrations; never large walls of text
- 🔒 **Every Gumloop-adjacent post IS marketing** — quality bar applies to all Gumloop communication
