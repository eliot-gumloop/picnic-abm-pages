## Usage Rules

### Display / hero headlines
- Very large, bold, fills the frame edge to edge
- Left-aligned
- Black on white or white on gradient
- Characters/icons can sit inline within the type flow

### Section labels
- ALL CAPS, small size, wide tracking, light gray
- Used to name content sections (e.g. "BRAND ETHOS", "BACKGROUND", "SECTION —")
- Placed top-left above the main heading

### Body copy
- Regular weight, short — max 2 paragraphs per surface
- Direct, plain language — "we keep it short and get to the point"
- Never dense blocks of text

### Scale hierarchy (visual from deck)
1. Hero display — fills full slide width, very large
2. Section heading — large, bold (e.g. "Announcements")
3. Body — small, regular, tight
4. Label — smallest, ALL CAPS, gray
